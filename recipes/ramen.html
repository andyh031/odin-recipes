All Natural Ramen Noodles

Description

I've tried those make your own ramen seasoning recipes and instant ramen noodles, but none of them bring out what ramen truly tastes like. This recipe hits home, and there is no msg nor artificial ingredients inside.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of vegetable broth
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon of Sriracha hot sauce
  • 9 ounces of soba noodles

Steps

  1. Combine the broth, water, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, and hot sauce in a pot.
  2. Bring to a boil.
  3. Add noodles to boiling broth mixture and cook until noodles are tender yet firm to the bite, which is around 5 to 7 minutes
  4. Transfer noodles to serving bowls and top with a desired amount of broth
